Privacy Policy

CharadeQuest - Fun Charades for Everyone!

Overview

CharadeQuest is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how our app handles your information.

CharadeQuest does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Your privacy is completely protected.

Data Collection

CharadeQuest does not collect any data from you. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or hardware information
  • Location data
  • Usage analytics or statistics
  • Crash reports or diagnostics
  • Any other form of user data

Offline Functionality

CharadeQuest works 100% offline. The app:

  • Does not require an internet connection to function
  • Does not connect to any external servers
  • Does not transmit any data over the network
  • Does not use any third-party analytics or tracking services

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Custom Categories: Any custom word categories you create are saved only on your device
  • Settings: Your game preferences (round duration, gesture settings, etc.) are stored locally
  • Favorites: Your favorite categories are remembered on your device

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties. If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

CharadeQuest is a family-friendly app suitable for all ages. Since we do not collect any personal information from any users, we are fully compliant with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

Third-Party Services

CharadeQuest does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or frameworks that collect user data. The app is completely self-contained.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Since CharadeQuest is designed to not collect data, any future updates will maintain this privacy-first approach.
