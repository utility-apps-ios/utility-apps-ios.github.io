How to Play

  1. Choose a Category - Pick from built-in categories organized by difficulty, or select one of your custom categories.
  2. Set the Timer - Choose your round duration: 30, 60, 90, or 120 seconds.
  3. Hold to Forehead - Hold your device to your forehead with the screen facing your friends so they can see the word.
  4. Act It Out - Your friends act out, describe, or give clues for the word without saying it!
  5. Tilt to Answer - When you guess correctly, tilt DOWN. To skip, tilt UP.
  6. See Your Score - When time's up, review how many you got right!

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my own category?

Go to Categories ‚Üí My Categories tab ‚Üí tap "Add Category". Enter a name and add your custom words one by one.

Can I edit or delete my custom categories?

Yes! Tap on any custom category to view it, then tap "Edit Word List" to add or remove words. You can also delete the entire category from the edit screen.

How do I mark a category as favorite?

Tap the star icon next to any category to mark it as a favorite. Favorites appear at the top of your category selection for quick access.

The tilt gestures aren't working. What should I do?

Make sure gestures are enabled in Settings. If they still don't work, you can use the on-screen Correct and Skip buttons instead.

Can I pause the game?

Yes! Tap the pause button in the center of the screen during gameplay to pause the timer. The word will be hidden while paused.

Does the app work without internet?

Yes! Charades Quest works 100% offline. All categories and your custom content are stored on your device.

Is the app safe for kids?

Absolutely! All built-in word lists are curated to be family-friendly and appropriate for all ages. We have a special "Kids" difficulty level with simple, child-appropriate words.

Why is there no sound on my iPad?

Make sure your device isn't in silent mode and the volume is turned up. Also check that Sound Effects are enabled in the app's Settings.

What happened to my custom categories after updating?

Custom categories are stored locally on your device and should persist through updates. If you deleted and reinstalled the app, local data would be lost. We recommend noting down important custom words.

Troubleshooting

App is running slowly

Try closing other apps running in the background and restart Charades Quest.

Gestures not responding correctly

  • Make sure you're holding the device steady before tilting
  • Tilt decisively - a quick, clear tilt works best
  • If issues persist, use the on-screen buttons instead (disable gestures in Settings)

Sound not playing

  • Check that your device is not in silent mode
  • Ensure volume is turned up
  • Verify Sound Effects are enabled in app Settings

App crashes or freezes

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
