üìù Working with Clips

Adding Clips

To add content to ClipStash:

  • Copy any text or image to your device's clipboard
  • Open ClipStash and tap the blue + button
  • Your clipboard content will be saved as a new clip

Smart Duplicate Detection: ClipStash automatically prevents duplicate content from being added to your collection.

Text Clips

Text clips display rich information about your copied text:

  • Word Count: See exactly how many words are in your text
  • Character Count: Track character length for social media posts
  • Preview: A preview of your text is shown in the list view
  • Full Content: Tap to view complete text in detail view

Image Clips

ClipStash fully supports image clipboard content:

  • Thumbnail Preview: See image previews in the list
  • Full Resolution: View full-size images in detail view
  • Share Integration: Easily share images to other apps

Troubleshooting

‚ùì "Nothing to add" message

This appears when your clipboard is empty. Please copy some text or an image, then tap the + button to save it to ClipStash.

‚ùì Duplicate clips not being prevented

ClipStash uses content-based hash detection. Very similar but not identical content may still be added as separate clips.

‚ùì Search not finding clips

Search only works with text content and titles. Images clips are not searchable by their visual content.

‚ùì Share not working

Ensure the receiving app supports the content type you're sharing. Some apps only accept specific formats.

üîí Privacy & Security

ClipStash stores all data locally on your device. Nothing is sent to external servers or cloud services. Your clipboard content remains private and secure.
