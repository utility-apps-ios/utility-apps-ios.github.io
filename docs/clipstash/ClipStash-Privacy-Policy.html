ClipStash does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All your clipboard data stays securely on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. ClipStash accesses your clipboard to save text and images you copy, storing all data locally on your device using iOS's secure data protection.

2. How We Use Your Information

Clipboard data is used solely to save and organize your clips, provide search functionality, enable copying back to clipboard, and manage favorites and filters.

ðŸ“± Local Storage Only

All your clipboard data is stored exclusively on your device using Core Data and iOS's built-in security features. We never upload, sync, or share your data with any external services.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share any information with third parties. ClipStash does not use analytics, advertisements, tracking, or cloud services.

4. Data Security

All data is protected by iOS's built-in encryption and never leaves your device. ClipStash operates entirely offline without internet connectivity.

5. Your Rights and Control

You have complete control over your data. Delete individual clips, clear all data through the app, or uninstall to remove everything. No account or personal information required.

6. Technical Information

Permissions Used: Clipboard access to read and save content you copy, and local storage to save your clipboard history on your device.

ClipStash does not request access to contacts, location, camera, microphone, or any other sensitive information.
