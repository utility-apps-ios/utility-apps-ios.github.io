Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2025

Introduction

AMC Math Practice ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Summary: We do not collect, store, or share any personal information. All your data stays on your device.

Information We Collect

AMC Math Practice is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect any personal information from you.

  • No account required - You can use the app without creating an account or providing any personal details
  • No personal data collected - We don't ask for your name, email, or any other personal information
  • No usage tracking - We don't track how you use the app

Data Storage

Your test history and results are stored locally on your device only.

  • Local storage only - All data remains on your device
  • No cloud sync - Your data is not uploaded to any server
  • No backups to our servers - We have no access to your test history
  • You control your data - Delete the app to remove all stored data, or use the in-app option to clear test history

Third-Party Services

The app connects to external services in limited ways:

  • AoPS Wiki - When you view a solution, the app loads content from the Art of Problem Solving Wiki (artofproblemsolving.com). This is an external website with its own privacy policy.

We do not use:

  • Analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media integrations
  • Crash reporting services that collect personal data

Children's Privacy

AMC Math Practice is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children
  • The app does not require any personal information to function
  • No social features, chat, or user-generated content
  • Parents can feel confident that their children can use this app safely

Data Security

Since we don't collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal security risk associated with using this app.

  • All data stays on your device
  • No data transmitted to our servers
  • Your device's built-in security protects your local data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes:

  • The updated policy will be posted on this page
  • The "Effective Date" at the top will be updated
  • For significant changes, we may notify you through the app

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
